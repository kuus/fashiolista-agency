A creative agency for influencers & brands

For brands

Fashiolista Agency connects brands to digital influencers. We are specialized in creating & executing A-Z concepts & campaigns for online and offline activations. Our focus is to get in touch with your target audience to realize engagement and interaction in a creative and effective way. All our work is custom made and created by our own in-house team of creatives, influence managers, designers and developers.

Request more information

For influencers

Fashiolista Agency represents an exclusive group of top influencers in the fashion, beauty and lifestyle industry. We connect them to the professional and commercial world by creating future work opportunities and provide management services. We will support and help them build their personal brand and digital personality.

Services

Some of our services include

  • Creating and managing various influencer collaborations: e.g. product placement, co-design, brand ambassadorship, editorial content, video content, contests, brand awareness campaigns
  • Concept, design, execution & strategy for online activations and creative campaigns
  • Creating and managing offline activations: e.g. Influencer trips, dinners and events
  • Social strategy: Facebook, Twitter, Instagram
  • Casting talent for (digital) ad campaigns, events or video/tv appearance

Fashiolista Agency initiatives include in-house concept development, design and execution. We report with details on traffic, social reach and consumer impact, identifying true ROI for influencer campaigns.

NikeID

Four influencers were selected as Nike’s official brand ambassadors and were asked to create a pair of NikeID sneakers. By sharing their experience, the influencers were engaging their audience in designing a pair too.

Influencers who worked on this

Stylescrapbook, Camille over the Rainbow, Mija, Lizzy vd Ligt, Adeline Rapon, Bisous Natasha

Philips City Scape

A group of international bloggers found fashion inspiration in music and mixed their Philips Citiscape headphones with their fashion week looks. Their audience and Fashiolista's community were able to interact with the looks. They could win a pair of headphones to take the challenge themselves.

Influencers who worked on this

Stylescrapbook, COTTDS, Kirilove, Liza Chloe, Just Like Sushi

My COS Style Icons

Four European top bloggers with the perfect aesthetic fit were connected to the Swedish brand COS. They were displayed as COS Style Icons and told the story of what represents ‘My COS’ by styling several outfits in their home & city environment. This collaboration was hosted on the blogs of the style icons only in a very discrete and authentic way. The icons showed how COS can be part of a day to day lifestyle.

Influencers who worked on this

Blame it on Fashion, Look de Pernille, Love Aesthetics, Fashionnerdic

MANGO Fashion Week Special

Kristina Bazan, the successful blogger behind fashion blog 'Kayture' was MANGO's official fashion week insider for 2013. She traveled to New York, Milan and Paris to cover runway shows for the Spanish fashion brand. Her looks and travel diary were featured on Fashiolista, where people could love and share their favorite fashion items. In an earlier collaboration with MANGO, we let ten influencers battle to win a trip to their show in Barcelona.

Influencers who worked on this

Kayture, Camille over the Rainbow, Natacha Steven, Modejunkie, Fashionata, The Golden Diamonds, Camille over the RainbowJemerced, Oh my Vogue, Mariannan, Pauline Fashion Blog, Fashion Fruit

L'Oréal Beauty Make-Over

We treated three beauty bloggers to a make-over by one of the top L’Oreal stylists in his salon. Their transformations were filmed and people could vote for their favorite new look to win a trip to Paris.

Influencers who worked on this

Miss Lipgloss, Beautygloss, Billierose.

Ace & Tate Brand Ambassador

London-based blogger Shini from Park & Cube promoted the launch of the Ace & Tate UK webshop with an editorial photoshoot on her blog. To help get the brand's name out there, give aways and promotion throughout Fashiolista was included.

Influencers who worked on this

Park & Cube and Tlnique

Fab. bag design

Three influencers were chosen to design their own limited edition Fab. bag. This resulted in a series of diverse bags that were sold internationally. After a huge 1st success, a 2nd edition with again three influencers was set up.

Influencers who worked on this

Kayture, The Fashionguitar, Ulrikke Lund, Mariannan, Alice Point, Jessica Christ

DKNY Looks

Let three bloggers style the DKNY new collection pieces in the Amsterdam city environment. A Style Off decided who was going to be the official DKNY Resort ambassador in de Bijenkorf warehouse.

Influencers who worked on this

Tlnique, Raspberry&Rouge, Lovely by Lucy

Reiss Spring '13

Four digital influencers styled their Reiss look and via a Style Off a winner was decided. The winning blogger was Silvia from Bartabac blog. She went to the REISS headquarters in London and styled her favorite spring looks.

Influencers who worked on this

Befrassy, Bartabac, Raspberry&Rouge, Denoto
